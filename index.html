Fortuna Terminal
A VT100 terminal emulator
  • PS/2 keyboard input
  • VGA output
  • Uses two ATMEGA-328P, one to receive keypresses and the other one to generate the video
  • Once it's ready, it will fit into an Altoids tin
  • Based on the Minimal-Terminal project

This was to be a Z80 computer whose project was abandoned.

It was suppose to support the addition of new board, expanding functionality. It was also supposed to be directly debugged from the computer.

It was too sophisticated for a first computer project, it grew too much in complexity and I ended abandoning the project, and moving to a much simpler alternative.
